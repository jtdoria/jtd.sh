jtd.sh

A personal domain for projects I tinker with on weekends.

Hobbyist code for fun and profit.

The current focus is on collecting my personal data and public data important to my daily life from as many sources as possible into a single tool for my own use. I'm connecting everything from local weather forecasts, my credit card transactions, my calendar, my to do lists, and so on to create tools that make my life easier to organize and track running on a local machine I own.

Just a guy trying to automate the boring parts of day to day life.
